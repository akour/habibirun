Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Habibi, Run! does not require an account or login. Gameplay records, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Advertising

The game uses Google Mobile Ads to offer an optional rewarded continue after a run ends. Google and its partners may process information such as device or advertising identifiers, IP-derived information, diagnostics, and ad interaction information to provide, measure, secure, and—depending on your consent and settings—personalize advertising.

Where required, a consent flow may be shown so you can make choices about advertising. The choices available depend on your region, device, and Google services configuration.

Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from apps that use its services.

Local game data

Run records, achievements, and settings are stored on your device. The game does not provide a cloud account or account-deletion system. Removing the app or clearing its storage may remove this local data.

Data retention

Local game data remains on your device until you clear the app's storage or uninstall it. Data processed by Google is retained under Google's applicable policies and settings.

Your choices

You can choose whether to use the optional rewarded continue. If an ad is unavailable, the game may offer the continue for free. Advertising and privacy choices may also be available through the consent flow, your device settings, and Google settings.

Children

Habibi, Run! is not directed specifically at children. Advertising and consent behavior follows the audience and regional settings configured for the published version of the game.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, email akour@habibiapps.com.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the game, its service providers, or legal requirements change. The date above identifies the latest version.
